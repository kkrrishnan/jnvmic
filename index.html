ABOUT THE SCHOOL

With the commitment to provide quality education based on Indian cultural and contemporary values and ethos. Lt. Dr. Ishwar Chandra Gupta and his brothers had the vision to establish the school in the fond memory of their father Late Sri Jai Narayan Gupta, the sole inspiration behind the school. In the year 1994 the total land area of around 7610 sq. mt. was acquired by 'JAI NARAYAN CHARITABLE TRUST' from the Kanpur Development Authority. The year 1996 was a landmark in the history of the school when Hon. Madhav Rao Devde laid the foundation stone of the school. After two years of tremendous efforts the three storeyed school building was made. The living and dynamic school system since then has established a name for itself in the city of Kanpur. Recognition: The school, from class 6th to 12th, science and commerce group is a coeducational institution. The school is affiliated to CBSE Board and recognised by Vidya Bharti.
