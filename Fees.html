OTHER INFORMATION REGARDING FEES :

1. The fee will be deposited in the prescribed bank between 11th to 25th. But if the fee is not deposited till the last date, the student will have to pay Rs. 25.00 will have to be deposited along with late fee by the end of the month. The entire due fee of one year can be deposited in one go.
2. If you leave the school in the middle of the session, fees will be charged for the period from July to December and if you leave from January or later, fees for the entire session will be charged. There is no provision for any discount or refund of fees in this.
3. Vehicle (bus) and cycle stand charges are not included in the fee. To avail this facility, fixed fee will be payable along with monthly fee. Arrangements can be made there only as per convenience!
